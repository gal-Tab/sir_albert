### 🔄 MOPs Work Management Flow

**1. Triage & Requests (👋 MOPA Requests - 2258528554)**
* **Flow:** Users fill a monday form ➡️ Ticket enters the `New items go here` group.
* **Review:** An Owner is assigned. The Owner reviews and decides the next step (Drop, Discovery, or move to Iteration Queue).

**2. Iteration Management (🏃‍♂️ MOPs iteration - 5582014840)**
* **Primary View:** "My active iteration" (View: 122602086).
* **Focus:** Gal is mainly interested in items where he is the `Owner` or `Stakeholder`.
* **Cadence:** Iterations are 2 weeks long (e.g., current is '2026 | Week 13-14', next is '2026 | Week 15-16').
* **Intake:** Items approved from the Requests board land in `New Item Go Here`. When ready, they move to the `Iteration Queue` or `Backlog` (which can be seen in the "Main table" view).

**3. Strategic Iteration Planning (🎯 Iteration Goals - 8600766067)**
* **Purpose:** Strategic items planned for the upcoming iteration (usually up to 3 items, sometimes more).
* **View:** View ID 185878550.

**4. Quarterly Roadmap (🧭 MOPA Roadmap 2026 - 5582014817)**
* **Purpose:** High-level, long-term strategic projects that span across an entire quarter.
* **Structure:** Grouped by Quarters (e.g., Q1 | 2026, Q2 | 2026).
