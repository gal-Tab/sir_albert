# MOPs Iteration Flow

## Overview
The internal marketing operations (MOPs) workflow used by Gal's team at monday.com. It relies on four primary interconnected boards to manage the lifecycle of tasks from triage to execution to quarterly planning.

## Key Components

1. **Triage & Requests:**
   - Board: 👋 MOPA Requests (2258528554)
   - Inbound forms drop tickets into "New items go here" for review and routing.

2. **Iteration Management:**
   - Board: 🏃‍♂️ MOPs iteration (5582014840)
   - Follows a 2-week cadence. Gal focuses primarily on items where he is the Owner or Stakeholder (View: "My active iteration").

3. **Strategic Iteration Planning:**
   - Board: 🎯 Iteration Goals (8600766067)
   - Tracks up to 3 strategic goals per upcoming sprint.

4. **Quarterly Roadmap:**
   - Board: 🧭 MOPA Roadmap 2026 (5582014817)
   - Groups major strategic projects by quarter (Q1, Q2, etc.).
